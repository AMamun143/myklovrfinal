Our Mission

MyKlovr's mission is to empower students and their parents to take ownership over their destiny and discover how their decisions today can impact their future potential, while providing schools and companies with multiple efficiency solutions.

We have created the ONLY all-purpose interactive self-serve proprietary data enabled recommendation platform which connects users to personalized tools, services, and resources to achieve their personal goals.
